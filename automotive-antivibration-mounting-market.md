<p><p>Automotive antivibration mounting systems come in two primary types: rubber and polyurethane. Polyurethane mounts, on the other hand, offer higher durability and resistance to chemicals and oil, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types of mounts play a crucial role in increasing driver comfort, reducing wear and tear on vehicle components, and improving overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
