<p><p>Liquid Crystalline Elastomers (LCEs) are a type of smart material that exhibit both elastomeric and liquid crystalline properties. There are two main types of LCE markets - Piezoelectric Elastomers and Optomechanic Elastomers. Piezoelectric Elastomers are capable of converting mechanical energy into electrical energy, making them suitable for sensors and energy harvesting applications. Optomechanic Elastomers, on the other hand, can change their optical properties in response to mechanical deformations, making them ideal for applications in optics and photonics.</p></p>
