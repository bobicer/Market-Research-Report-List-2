<p><ul><li>Portable Type</li><li>Standby Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household generators come in two main types: portable and standby. Portable generators are compact, mobile units that can be easily moved around and used to power essential appliances during power outages or outdoor activities. Standby generators, on the other hand, are permanently installed outside the home and automatically turn on during power failures, providing continuous backup power. While portable generators are more affordable and versatile, standby generators offer a seamless and convenient solution for homes that require uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
