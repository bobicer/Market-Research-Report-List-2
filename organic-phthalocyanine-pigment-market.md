<p><p>Organic Phthalocyanine Pigments are classified into two main types: Metal Phthalocyanine Pigments and Metal Free Phthalocyanine Pigments. Metal Phthalocyanine Pigments incorporate metal ions such as copper, cobalt, or nickel in their molecular structure, while Metal Free Phthalocyanine Pigments do not contain any metal ions. Both types of pigments are widely used in various applications such as printing inks, coatings, plastics, and textiles due to their excellent color strength, stability, and lightfastness.</p></p>
