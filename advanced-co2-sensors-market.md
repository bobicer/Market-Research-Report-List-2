<p><p>Advanced CO2 sensors are essential for monitoring air quality in various industries and applications. Two main types of CO2 sensors are NDIR (non-dispersive infrared) and chemical sensors. NDIR sensors measure CO2 levels by detecting the absorption of infrared light, offering high accuracy and reliability. Chemical sensors, on the other hand, detect CO2 through chemical reactions, making them suitable for specific applications. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring indoor air quality and can be found in industries such as HVAC, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
