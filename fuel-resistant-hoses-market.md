<p><p>Fuel-resistant hoses are categorized into two types, Type I and Type II. Type I hoses are designed for use with gasoline and diesel fuels, as well as biodiesel blends up to B20. These hoses are suitable for general-purpose applications and have a maximum working pressure of 50 PSI. On the other hand, Type II hoses are specifically formulated for use with more aggressive fuels, such as ethanol blends up to E85. These hoses have a higher working pressure of 65 PSI and are ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring greater chemical resistance.</p></p>
