<p><p>Temperature calibration equipment market can be categorized based on their types - Benchtop Type and Portable Type. They are suitable for calibration of high-accuracy instruments and can handle a wide range of temperature inputs. On the other hand, Portable Type equipment is smaller in size and designed for on-site calibration needs or fieldwork.
