<p><p>Photonic detectors are used for detecting light in various applications. There are two main types of photonic detectors - photoconductive detectors and photovoltaic detectors. </p><p>Photoconductive detectors operate by changing their electrical conductivity in response to light, allowing them to detect light intensity or other characteristics. </p><p>On the other hand, photovoltaic detectors generate electricity when exposed to light, converting the light energy into electrical signals for detection. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in various industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
