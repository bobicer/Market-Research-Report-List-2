<p><ul><li>Communications System</li><li>Navigation System</li><li>Display System</li><li>Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Avionics instruments can be categorized into four main types: Communications System, Navigation System, Display System, and Management System. The Communications System includes devices that enable communication between the aircraft and ground stations or other aircraft. The Navigation System consists of instruments that help in determining the aircraft's position and direction during flight. The Display System provides pilots with crucial information such as altitude, airspeed, and engine status. The Management System includes tools for monitoring and controlling various systems within the aircraft.</p></p>
