<p><p>Agricultural fogging machines are used to disperse insecticides, fungicides, and other chemicals in agricultural settings. Thermal agricultural fogging machines use heat to create a fog, while cold agricultural fogging machines use compressed air to create a fog without heat. Thermal machines are often used in cooler climates or for larger areas, while cold machines are preferred in warmer climates or for more targeted applications. Both types of machines are essential tools for controlling pests and diseases in agricultural crops.</p></p>
