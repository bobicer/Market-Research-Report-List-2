<p><p>Linear Low Density Polyethylene Resins market can be classified into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I market refers to resins that are primarily used in film applications like packaging and agricultural films. These resins have high tensile strength and puncture resistance. On the other hand, Type II market includes resins used in injection molding and rotational molding applications. These resins have better impact strength and are commonly used in industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
