<p><p>An Inertial Navigation System (INS) for vehicles is a navigation aid that uses accelerometers and gyroscopes to continuously calculate the vehicle's position, velocity, and orientation. This system does not rely on external references such as GPS signals, making it particularly useful in environments where GPS signals may be obstructed or unavailable, such as tunnels, urban canyons, or indoor settings.</p><p>The Inertial Navigation System for Vehicle Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for accurate real-time navigation systems in vehicles, particularly in autonomous vehicles, is driving the growth of the market. The advancements in sensor technologies, MEMS-based inertial sensors, and software algorithms are also fueling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of INS in commercial vehicles, military applications, and unmanned aerial vehicles is further contributing to the market expansion. The integration of inertial navigation systems with other sensors such as cameras, LiDAR, and radar for enhanced navigation capabilities is a key trend in the market.
