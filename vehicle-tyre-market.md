<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial tyres are constructed with ply cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing greater stability, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. They are widely used in passenger cars and trucks. Bias tyres, on the other hand, have ply cords that run diagonally across the tyre, offering better load-carrying capacity and durability. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles such as off-road trucks and construction equipment. Both types of tyres have their distinct advantages and are tailored to different market segments based on specific requirements.</p></p>
