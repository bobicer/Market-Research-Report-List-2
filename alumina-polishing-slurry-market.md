<p><p>Alumina polishing slurry comes in two main types: Nano Grade and Micron Grade. Nano Grade slurry contains nanoparticles of alumina, which are extremely fine and have a particle size of less than 100 nanometers. This type of slurry is ideal for achieving high-precision polishing results. On the other hand, Micron Grade slurry contains larger particles, typically ranging from 0.1 to 10 microns in size. This type of slurry is commonly used for more general polishing applications where a high level of precision is not required.</p></p>
