<p><ul><li>Evaporative Crystallizers</li><li>Cooling Crystallizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Crystallizer Market Types are Evaporative Crystallizers and Cooling Crystallizers. Evaporative Crystallizers operate by heating a solution until the solvent evaporates, leaving behind the crystallized product. These are commonly used in industries such as chemical processing and wastewater treatment. Cooling Crystallizers work by cooling a solution to lower its solubility, causing crystals to form. They are often used in the food and pharmaceutical industries. Both types play a crucial role in separating and purifying solids from liquids in industrial processes.</p></p>
