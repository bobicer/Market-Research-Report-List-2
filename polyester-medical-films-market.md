<p><ul><li>Under 100 μm</li><li>100-300 μm</li><li>Above 300 μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester medical films are categorized into three types based on their thickness. Under 100 μm films are commonly used for applications requiring flexibility and conformability, such as wound dressings and drug delivery systems. Films with thickness ranging from 100-300 μm are suitable for applications like medical imaging and diagnostics where a balance of flexibility and strength is important. Films above 300 μm are typically used in applications requiring high strength and durability, such as medical packaging and surgical drapes.</p></p>
