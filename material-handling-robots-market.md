<p><ul><li>Articulated Material Handling Robots</li><li>SCARA Material Handling Robot</li><li>Parallel Material Handling Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Material handling robots are categorized into different types based on their mechanical structure and capabilities. Articulated material handling robots have multiple joints and can move in a wide range of motion, making them versatile for various tasks. SCARA material handling robots have a vertical arm and can move in a circular motion, making them suitable for assembly and pick-and-place operations. Parallel material handling robots have multiple arms that work in parallel, allowing for increased speed and precision in handling tasks. Each type offers specific benefits for different applications in the market.</p></p>
