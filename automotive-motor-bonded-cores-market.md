<p><p>Automotive motor bonded cores are essential components in the manufacturing of electric motors. The two main types of cores used in these applications are cold rolled non-oriented (CRNO) and cold rolled grain-oriented (CRGO) cores. CRNO cores are widely used in smaller, low-powered motors, while CRGO cores are preferred for larger, higher-efficiency motors due to their superior magnetic properties. Both types play a crucial role in the performance and efficiency of automotive motors, making them key elements in the market.</p></p>
