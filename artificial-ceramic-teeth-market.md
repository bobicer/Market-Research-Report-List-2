<p><p>The artificial ceramic teeth market can be divided into three main types: all-ceramic, metal-ceramic, and other materials. All-ceramic teeth are made entirely of ceramic material, providing a natural look and high biocompatibility. Metal-ceramic teeth have a metal substructure covered with ceramic, combining strength and aesthetics. The "other" market includes various materials such as zirconia and composite resins. Each type offers different advantages in terms of durability, aesthetics, and cost, allowing for tailored solutions for individual patient needs.</p></p>
