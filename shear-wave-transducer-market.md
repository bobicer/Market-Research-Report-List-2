<p><strong>The Shear Wave Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Incidence Sensor</li><li>Radial Incidence Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shear Wave Transducer Market includes two main types: Normal Incidence Sensor and Radial Incidence Sensor. Normal Incidence Sensor uses transducers placed perpendicular to the surface being tested, allowing for accurate detection of flaws in the material. Radial Incidence Sensor involves transducers placed at an angle to the surface, providing a different perspective for flaw detection. Both types cater to specific testing needs and offer unique benefits for different industries and applications within the non-destructive testing market.</p></p>
