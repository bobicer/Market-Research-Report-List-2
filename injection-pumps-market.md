<p><strong>The Injection Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Distributor Pump</li><li>Individual Control Pump</li><li>Unit Injection</li><li>Common Rail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection pumps are used in diesel engines to deliver fuel to the cylinders at the correct time and in the right quantity. The types of injection pumps include rotary distributor pump, individual control pump, unit injection, and common rail system. A rotary distributor pump distributes fuel to each cylinder sequentially, while an individual control pump allows independent control of fuel delivery to each cylinder. Unit injection pumps combine the injector and pump into a single unit, while common rail systems use a high-pressure rail to supply fuel to multiple injectors simultaneously.</p></p>
