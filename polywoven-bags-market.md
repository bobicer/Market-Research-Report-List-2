<p><ul><li>Liner Polywoven Bags</li><li>Non-Liner Polywoven Bags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polywoven bags are categorized into liner and non-liner types based on their construction. Liner polywoven bags have an additional inner layer for protection against moisture and external factors, making them ideal for storing food and agricultural products. On the other hand, non-liner polywoven bags do not have this inner layer, making them more suitable for packaging heavy-duty items like construction materials and chemicals. Both types cater to different market needs and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the products being stored or transported.</p></p>
