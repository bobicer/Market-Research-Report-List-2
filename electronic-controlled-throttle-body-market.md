<p><p>The Electronic Controlled Throttle Body market consists of three main types of components: Actuator, Throttle Plate, and Throttle Position Sensor. The Actuator is responsible for opening and closing the Throttle Plate to control air flow to the engine. The Throttle Plate regulates the amount of air entering the engine. The Throttle Position Sensor provides feedback to the engine control unit on the position of the throttle plate. These components work together in the Electronic Controlled Throttle Body system to optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
