<p><p>Robotic deburring machines are categorized into different types based on their deburring processes. Rotary Transfer Deburring machines utilize a rotary transfer system to remove burrs from workpieces. High Pressure Deburring machines employ high-pressure fluids to eliminate burrs. Ultrasonic Deburring machines use ultrasonic vibrations to conduct deburring. Other types of deburring machines may include laser deburring or abrasive blasting machines. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities for effectively removing burrs from various materials.</p></p>
