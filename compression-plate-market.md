<p><p>Compression plates are orthopedic devices used to stabilize and support fractured bones during the healing process. In the adult market, compression plates are designed for full-grown individuals with mature bone structures. They are typically larger and stronger to accommodate the higher force loads experienced by adults. In contrast, pediatric compression plates are specifically tailored for children and adolescents with developing bone structures. They are smaller in size and more flexible to provide the necessary support without impeding growth. Both types of compression plates play a crucial role in promoting proper bone healing in different patient populations.</p></p>
