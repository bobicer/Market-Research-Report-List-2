<p><p>Printing consumables market includes two main types - inkjet consumables and laser consumables. Inkjet consumables consist of ink cartridges that are used in inkjet printers to create high-quality color prints. Laser consumables, on the other hand, include toner cartridges used in laser printers to produce sharp black and white prints. Both types of consumables play a crucial role in the printing industry by providing essential supplies for different types of printers to ensure high-quality and consistent printing results.</p></p>
