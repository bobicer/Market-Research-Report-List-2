<p><ul><li>Contact Points 8 - 12</li><li>Contact Points blow 8</li><li>Contact Points above 12</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microelectrodes are classified based on the number of contact points they have. Contact points 8-12 electrodes are commonly used for neurophysiological studies due to their balanced sensitivity and selectivity. Contact points below 8 are preferred for more localized measurements, while contact points above 12 are used for broader spatial sampling. Each type caters to specific research needs, offering varying degrees of precision and coverage for different applications in neuroscience and biomedical research.</p></p>
