<p><p>Selective herbicides target specific types of plants while leaving other plants unharmed, making them ideal for weed control in lawns, gardens, and crops. Non-selective herbicides, on the other hand, kill all vegetation they come into contact with and are commonly used for clearing weeds in driveways, sidewalks, and other non-crop areas. Both types serve important roles in the preemergent herbicide market.</p></p>
