<p><p>Electric heating cable market is classified into four types: self-regulating electric heating cable, constant wattage electric heating cable, mineral insulated electric heating cable, and skin-effect electric heating cable. </p><p>Self-regulating electric heating cable adjusts its power output based on temperature changes. Constant wattage electric heating cable provides a consistent power output. Mineral insulated electric heating cable is highly durable and can withstand extreme temperatures. Skin-effect electric heating cable is designed for high power transmission in industrial applications. Each type offers unique features and benefits for specific heating requirements.</p></p>
