<p><p>Multi-axis articulated robot arms are categorized into dual axis and single axis (above two) based on the number of articulation points they possess. Dual axis arms have two points of articulation, allowing them to move in two directions. Single axis arms with more than two articulation points can move in multiple directions, making them more versatile and suitable for complex tasks. Both types of robot arms are used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics for automation and assembly purposes.</p></p>
