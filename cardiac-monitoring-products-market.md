<p><p>Cardiac monitoring products market includes various types such as Resting ECG, Stress ECG, Holter ECG, and others. Resting ECG involves monitoring the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG measures the heart's response to physical activity or stress. Holter ECG allows continuous monitoring of the heart's activity over a period of time. Other types of cardiac monitoring products include event monitors, arrhythmia monitors, and implantable loop recorders, which serve specific purposes in tracking and diagnosing cardiac conditions.</p></p>
