<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) come in different types such as X7R, X5R, C0G (NP0), Y5V, and Others. X7R and X5R types are known for their stability over a wide range of temperatures. Each type serves specific purposes based on their unique electrical properties.</p></p>
