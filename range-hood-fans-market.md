<p><strong>The Range Hood Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wall-Chimney Hood</li><li>Under-Cabinet Hood</li><li>Island Hood</li><li>Downdraft Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Range hood fans are available in various types to suit different kitchen layouts and designs. Wall-Chimney hoods are mounted on the wall above the cooking surface, while Under-Cabinet hoods are installed beneath cabinets. Island hoods are ideal for kitchens with an island cooktop, as they are mounted from the ceiling. Downdraft hoods are great for kitchens where venting through the ceiling or wall is not possible, as they draw smoke and steam down through ducts below the floor. Each type of range hood fan offers unique benefits and functionality to enhance kitchen ventilation.</p></p>
