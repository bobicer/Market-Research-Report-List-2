<p><p>Hydropneumatic tanks are used in water systems to regulate pressure and maintain water supply. There are two main types of hydropneumatic tanks - bladder type and diaphragm type. Bladder type tanks have a flexible membrane that separates water from air, while diaphragm type tanks use a rigid barrier. Both types serve the same purpose of storing water under pressure to ensure a steady flow throughout the system. They are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
