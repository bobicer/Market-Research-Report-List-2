<p><p>Folding boxboards market types include coating, bleached chemical pulp, mechanical pulp, and unbleached chemical pulp. Coating refers to the surface treatment of the board for improved printability and appearance. Bleached chemical pulp is made from wood fibers that are chemically processed to remove impurities and create a brighter material. Mechanical pulp is produced by grinding wood fibers without chemical treatment, resulting in a less refined product. Unbleached chemical pulp is similar to bleached pulp but retains its natural color and may have different strength and appearance properties.</p></p>
