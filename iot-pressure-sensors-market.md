<p><p>The IoT pressure sensors market offers various types of sensors to cater to different pressure measurement needs. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, gauge pressure sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, vacuum pressure sensors measure pressures below atmospheric pressure, differential pressure sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, and sealed pressure sensors are designed to withstand harsh environments. Each type of sensor serves a specific purpose and is utilized in various applications across industries.</p></p>
