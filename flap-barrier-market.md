<p><p>Flap Barrier market can be categorized into Single Movement and Double Movement types based on their operation. Single Movement flap barriers have a single flap arm that rotates in one direction to allow entry or exit. On the other hand, Double Movement flap barriers have two flap arms that move in opposite directions to regulate the flow of people. Both types offer efficient and secure access control solutions for various environments such as offices, airports, and public transportation hubs.</p></p>
