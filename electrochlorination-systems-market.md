<p><p>Electrochlorination systems are used for generating sodium hypochlorite solutions, which are widely used for water disinfection in various industries. The two main types of electrochlorination systems are brine systems and seawater systems. Brine systems use saltwater as the source of chlorine, while seawater systems directly convert seawater into chlorine. Brine systems are more commonly used in industries that have access to freshwater sources, while seawater systems are preferred in coastal areas or marine applications where the availability of seawater is abundant.</p></p>
