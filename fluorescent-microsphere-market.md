<p><p>Fluorescent microspheres are commonly classified into two main types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are typically made of a shell-like structure that is lighter and more buoyant, making them suitable for applications such as medical imaging and drug delivery. Both types of microspheres have unique characteristics and properties that make them valuable in various fields of research and technology.</p></p>
