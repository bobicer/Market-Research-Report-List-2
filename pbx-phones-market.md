<p><p>PBX phones come in two types: multi-line and single-line. Multi-line PBX phones allow multiple users to share the same phone system and have their own designated lines for making and receiving calls. This is commonly used in businesses with multiple employees. On the other hand, single-line PBX phones are designed for individual use and have only one line for one user. They are commonly used in home offices or by individuals who do not need to share a phone system.</p></p>
