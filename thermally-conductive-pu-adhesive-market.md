<p><p>Thermally conductive PU adhesive market types include single component and multi-component options. Single-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, simplifying the application process. They are convenient and ideal for quick bonding jobs. On the other hand, multi-component adhesives require mixing of two or more components before application. They offer better control over curing time and bond strength, making them suitable for more complex projects.
