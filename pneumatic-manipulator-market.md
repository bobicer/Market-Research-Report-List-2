<p><ul><li>Hanging</li><li>Column</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic manipulators come in two main types: hanging and column. Hanging manipulators are suspended from an overhead structure, allowing for easy movement and reach in various directions. They are commonly used in assembly and packaging applications. On the other hand, column manipulators are fixed to a vertical column or post, providing stability and precision for lifting and positioning heavy objects. They are often used in industries such as automotive and metalworking for heavy-duty tasks. Both types offer efficient and ergonomic solutions for material handling.</p></p>
