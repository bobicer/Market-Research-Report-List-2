<p><p>Diabetic Neuropathy Treatment Market consists of different types including Peripheral Neuropathy, Autonomic Neuropathy, Proximal Neuropathy, and Focal Neuropathy. Peripheral Neuropathy affects the nerves in the extremities, Autonomic Neuropathy affects the nerves that control involuntary bodily functions, Proximal Neuropathy affects the nerves in the hips, buttocks, or thighs, while Focal Neuropathy affects a single nerve or group of nerves. Treatment options for these conditions may include medication, physical therapy, lifestyle changes, and in severe cases, surgery.</p></p>
