PMOLED displays are simpler and cheaper to produce, making them ideal for small screens like wearable devices. In contrast, AMOLED displays offer higher resolution, brightness, and efficiency, making them suitable for larger screens like smartphones and TVs. The AMOLED market is larger and more competitive, while PMOLED is still relevant for niche applications. Both markets continue to grow and evolve with advancing technology.</p></p>
