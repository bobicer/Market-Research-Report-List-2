<p><p>Biobased products can be divided into four main market types: biogas, fuel ethanols, biodiesels, and other biobased products. Biogas is produced through anaerobic digestion of organic matter and can be used as a renewable energy source. Fuel ethanols are typically derived from crops like corn or sugarcane and are used as an alternative to traditional gasoline. Biodiesels are made from vegetable oils or animal fats and can be used as a sustainable fuel for vehicles. Other biobased products include bioplastics, bio-based chemicals, and bio-based lubricants.</p></p>
