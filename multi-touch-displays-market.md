<p><p>Multi-touch displays can be categorized into different types based on their technology. Resistive multi-touch displays consist of several layers that respond to pressure, making them ideal for precise interactions with styluses or gloves. Capacitive multi-touch displays use electrical currents to sense touch, enabling faster and more accurate input with fingers. Other types of multi-touch displays include infrared, optical, acoustic, and force-sensitive variations, each offering unique benefits for specific use cases and requirements.</p></p>
