<p><p>Car horns are an essential safety feature in vehicles, with two main types dominating the market: air horns and electric horns. Air horns use compressed air to create a loud sound and are typically found in larger vehicles like trucks and trains. Electric horns, on the other hand, use an electric current to produce a sharp sound and are more common in smaller vehicles like cars and motorcycles. Both types serve the same purpose of alerting other drivers and pedestrians of potential dangers on the road.</p></p>
