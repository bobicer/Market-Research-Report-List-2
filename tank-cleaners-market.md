<p><p>Tank cleaners are commonly classified into two main types based on their pH level: Neutral and Alkaline cleaners. Neutral cleaners have a pH level close to 7, making them suitable for general cleaning tasks without causing damage to the tank surfaces or equipment. On the other hand, alkaline cleaners have a higher pH level, typically above 7, making them effective at removing tough stains and contaminants from tanks. However, they can potentially cause damage to certain materials if not used properly.</p></p>
