<p><strong>The Depth Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth filters are commonly used in two main market types: Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration) and Bottom-In-Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration). </p><p>In the Inline Configuration, the fluid enters from the bottom and flows through the filter media in a top-to-bottom direction. On the other hand, in the T-Configuration, the fluid enters from the bottom, passes through the filter media, and exits from the bottom as well. Both configurations are utilized depending on the specific application and filtration requirements.</p></p>
