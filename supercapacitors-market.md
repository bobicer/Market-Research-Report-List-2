<p><strong>The Supercapacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Layer</li><li>Pseudocapacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supercapacitors can be categorized into two main types based on their energy storage mechanisms: Double Layer Capacitors (DLCs) and Pseudocapacitors. DLCs store energy through electrostatic double-layer formation at the electrode-electrolyte interface, providing high power density and long cycle life. Pseudocapacitors, on the other hand, store energy through fast and reversible redox reactions at the electrode surface, offering higher energy density than DLCs. Both types of supercapacitors have unique advantages and are utilized in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
