<p><p>Floor sandwich panels are building materials that consist of two thin, rigid facings bonded to a lightweight core. These panels are commonly categorized based on the type of facing material used. The three main market types for floor sandwich panels are aluminum face, fiberglass face, and other. Aluminum face panels are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for industrial applications. Fiberglass face panels are lightweight and offer good insulation properties. The "other" market includes panels with facings made of materials such as steel, wood, or PVC. Each type of facing material offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
