<p><p>Temperature monitoring market can be categorized into two types, namely, non-contact temperature sensors and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors work by measuring the infrared radiation emitted by an object, while contact temperature sensors require physical contact with the object to measure its temperature. Non-contact temperature sensors are preferred in applications where contact may not be possible or desirable, such as in food processing or medical settings. Contact temperature sensors are commonly used in industrial processes, HVAC systems, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
