<p><ul><li>Two-Component Epoxy Mortar</li><li>Three-Component Epoxy Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy mortar market consists of two main types: Two-component epoxy mortar and Three-component epoxy mortar. Two-component epoxy mortar is a mixture of resin and hardener that cures when mixed. It is easy to use and widely available. On the other hand, Three-component epoxy mortar includes resin, hardener, and aggregate that provides high strength and durability. This type is ideal for heavy-duty applications where superior strength and chemical resistance are required. Both types offer excellent bond strength and are commonly used for flooring, repairs, and construction projects.</p></p>
