<p><p>Rigid Inflatable Boats (RIBs) are commonly classified into two main types based on their tube design - Double Tube Boat RIBs and Single Tube Boat RIBs. Double Tube Boat RIBs have two separate inflatable tubes running along the sides of the boat, providing extra buoyancy, stability, and impact resistance. Single Tube Boat RIBs, on the other hand, have a single inflatable tube that surrounds the boat, offering a simpler design and easier maneuverability. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of the users in the marine industry.</p></p>
