<p><strong>The Marine Engine Fuel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle System</li><li>Common Rail System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine engine fuel injection system market is segmented into three main types: pump-line-nozzle system, common rail system, and others. The pump-line-nozzle system involves a separate pump for each cylinder, which directly injects fuel through individual fuel lines and nozzles. The common rail system uses a high-pressure fuel rail to supply fuel to all injectors simultaneously. The "others" category includes alternative fuel injection systems that may employ different technologies or designs to deliver fuel to the engine.</p></p>
