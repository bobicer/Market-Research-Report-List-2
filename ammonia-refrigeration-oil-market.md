<p><p>Ammonia refrigeration oil is a type of lubricant used in industrial refrigeration systems that utilize ammonia as the refrigerant. This has led to a higher adoption of ammonia refrigeration systems, thereby boosting the demand for ammonia refrigeration oil.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of high-performance lubricants that offer better efficiency and longevity to refrigeration systems.
