<p><p>Polyethylene Oxide Film market can be categorized into three types based on their properties and characteristics. Instant Film refers to fast-dissolving films that are used for quick release of active ingredients. Medium Soluble Membrane films dissolve at a moderate rate and are commonly used in controlled release applications. On the other hand, Hard to Crystallize Film market consists of films that are difficult to crystallize, making them suitable for applications requiring high clarity and flexibility. Each type caters to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
