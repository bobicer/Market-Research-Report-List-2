<p><p>Commercial dough dividers come in two main types: Fully-Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Fully-Automatic dough dividers require minimal human intervention as they can measure, divide, and round the dough automatically. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic dividers require some manual effort in terms of measuring and feeding the dough into the machine, but the machine takes care of the dividing and rounding process. Both types of dividers are widely used in bakeries and food production facilities to save time and ensure uniform dough portions.</p></p>
