<p><p>E Clinical Solution Software Market includes two main types: Clinical Trial Management System (CTMS) and Clinical Data Management System (CDMS). </p><p>CTMS software helps in planning, tracking, and managing clinical trials efficiently. It streamlines processes such as participant enrollment, scheduling, and monitoring of study progress.</p><p>CDMS software, on the other hand, organizes and stores clinical trial data in a structured manner. It ensures data integrity, accuracy, and compliance with regulatory requirements, making it easier for researchers to analyze and report findings.</p></p>
