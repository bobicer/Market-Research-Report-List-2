<p><p>HVDC valves are essential components in high-voltage direct current transmission systems. There are two main types of HVDC valves in the market: Conventional DC Valves and Flexible DC Valves. Conventional DC valves are the traditional type that control the flow of electricity in a straightforward manner. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the reliable and efficient transmission of electricity over long distances.</p></p>
