<p><p>Metallocene Polypropylene (mPP) is a type of polypropylene produced using metallocene catalysts, resulting in improved properties like high clarity, high stiffness, and excellent impact resistance. There are two main types of mPP based on the polymer structure: Metallocene Isotactic Polypropylene (miPP) and Metallocene Syndiotactic Polypropylene (msPP). miPP has a regular and uniform polymer structure, offering high tensile strength and easy processability, while msPP has a unique alternating polymer structure, providing enhanced toughness and impact resistance. These different types cater to various applications in industries like packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
