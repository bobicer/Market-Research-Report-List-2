<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction equipment tires come in three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a cushioning effect to absorb shocks from rough terrain. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and are puncture-proof, making them ideal for heavy-duty construction work. Polyurethane tires are lightweight and offer good durability and resistance to wear and tear. Each type of tire is suited for different construction needs and environments, providing various benefits for equipment operators.</p></p>
