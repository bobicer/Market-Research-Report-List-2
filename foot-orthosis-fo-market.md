<p><p>Foot Orthosis (FO) Market consists of two main types: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics use a moving component to provide support and assist with foot movement, often used for conditions requiring active movement. Static Orthotics use a rigid design to provide support and stability, commonly used for conditions requiring immobilization. Both types aim to improve foot function, reduce pain, and promote proper alignment. The choice between Dynamic or Static Orthotics depends on the individual's specific needs and condition.</p></p>
