<p><p>Dock gangways come in two main types: with handrails and without handrails. Gangways with handrails provide extra safety and security for users, especially in rough or slippery conditions. They are preferred in high-traffic areas or where the risk of falls is higher. On the other hand, gangways without handrails are more lightweight and portable, making them suitable for temporary or low-traffic use. Both types serve the same purpose of providing safe passage between a dock and a vessel.</p></p>
