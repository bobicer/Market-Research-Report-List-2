<p><p>Welding Equipment Market is segmented into various types such as Arc Welding, Electrogas/Electroslag Welding, Friction Welding, Laser & Electron-Beam Welding, Resistance Welding, Oxy-Fuel Welding, and Others. Arc welding involves using electricity to create an arc between the electrode and the materials being welded. Electrogas/electroslag welding uses gas or slag to protect the weld from contaminants. Friction welding involves joining materials through friction-induced heat. Laser & electron-beam welding uses high energy beams to melt and join materials. Resistance welding involves using electricity to heat and join materials together. Oxy-fuel welding uses a mixture of fuel gas and oxygen to create a flame for welding. Other types of welding equipment include ultrasonic, plasma, and explosive welding.</p></p>
