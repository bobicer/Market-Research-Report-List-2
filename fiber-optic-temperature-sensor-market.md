<p><ul><li>Distributed Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor</li><li>Fiber Optic Fluorescence Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic temperature sensors come in different types, with two main categories being distributed fiber optic temperature sensors and fiber optic fluorescence temperature sensors. Distributed fiber optic sensors allow for measuring temperature along the entire length of the fiber, providing continuous monitoring. On the other hand, fiber optic fluorescence sensors use a fluorescent material that changes its properties with temperature, allowing for precise temperature measurements. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and are used in various industries for temperature monitoring applications.</p></p>
