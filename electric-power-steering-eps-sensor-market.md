<p><p>Electric Power Steering EPS Sensor market is categorized into two main types - Torque Only Sensor (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensor (TAS). TOS measures the amount of steering force applied by the driver, while TAS not only measures the torque but also the steering angle. TOS sensors are simpler and more cost-effective, while TAS sensors provide additional information for better steering precision and control. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and responsive electric power steering systems in vehicles.</p></p>
