<p><p>Conveyor belt loader market consists of two main types: self-propelled and towable models. Self-propelled conveyor belt loaders are equipped with their own engine and can move independently around an airport or other location. Towable models, on the other hand, need to be towed by a vehicle such as a baggage tractor. Both types are widely used in airports and other industries for loading and unloading luggage, cargo, and other materials onto airplanes or trucks.</p></p>
