<p><ul><li>Alkali Silicate Water borne</li><li>Ethyl Silicate Solvent borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inorganic Zinc Coatings are divided into two main types: Alkali Silicate Water borne and Ethyl Silicate Solvent borne coatings. The Alkali Silicate Water borne coatings are environmentally friendly and have low VOC content, making them ideal for use in water-based formulations. On the other hand, Ethyl Silicate Solvent borne coatings are solvent-based and offer superior chemical and abrasion resistance. Both types of coatings provide excellent corrosion protection for various substrates, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
