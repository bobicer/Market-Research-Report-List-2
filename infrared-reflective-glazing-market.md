<p><p>In the Infrared Reflective Glazing market, there are two main types: Direct-Coat IRR Glazing and IRR Glazing with IR Film. Direct-Coat IRR Glazing involves applying a special coating directly onto the glass to provide infrared reflective properties. On the other hand, IRR Glazing with IR Film involves applying a thin film containing infrared reflective properties onto the glass surface. Both types of glazing aim to reduce heat and UV radiation entering the building, improving energy efficiency and comfort for occupants.</p></p>
