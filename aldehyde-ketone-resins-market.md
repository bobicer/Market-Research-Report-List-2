<p><p>Aldehyde Ketone Resins have two main market types: Photopolymerization and Thermal Polymerization. Photopolymerization involves using light to initiate a chemical reaction, leading to the formation of a polymer. This process is commonly used in industries such as printing and adhesives. On the other hand, Thermal Polymerization relies on heat to trigger the polymerization reaction, resulting in the formation of a resin. This method is often used in industries such as coatings and sealants. Both market types cater to different applications and industries, providing a wide range of options for consumers.</p></p>
