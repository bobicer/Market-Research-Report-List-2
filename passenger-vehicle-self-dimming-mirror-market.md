<p><p>Passenger vehicle self-dimming mirrors come in two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are primarily located on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically adjust to reduce glare from headlights behind the vehicle. Inside self-dimming mirrors are located on the interior of the vehicle and perform a similar function by automatically dimming to improve visibility for the driver. Both types of self-dimming mirrors enhance driving safety and convenience by reducing glare and improving overall visibility.</p></p>
