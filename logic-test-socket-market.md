<p><p>The Logic Test Socket market is segmented into various types including PoP (Package on Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead), FBGA (Fine-Pitch Ball Grid Array), SOP (Small Outline Package), and Others. PoP sockets are used for stacking multiple integrated circuits in a compact space. QFN sockets are commonly used for their small size and high thermal performance. FBGA sockets provide a high level of reliability and signal integrity. SOP sockets are used for their simplicity and low cost. Other types of sockets cater to specific requirements for testing different types of logic devices.</p></p>
