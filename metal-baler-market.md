<p><p>Metal balers are machines used to compress and bundle scrap metal for easy transportation and recycling. There are two main types of metal balers in the market: manual and PLC automatic. Manual balers require human input to operate, while PLC automatic balers are equipped with programmable logic controllers that automate the compression and bundling process. While manual balers are more cost-effective, PLC automatic balers offer greater efficiency and convenience in handling large volumes of scrap metal. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
