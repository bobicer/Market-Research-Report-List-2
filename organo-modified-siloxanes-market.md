<p><p>Organo-modified siloxanes are widely used in various industries due to their unique properties. These siloxanes can have different structures, such as comb-like, linear, or a combination of both. Comb-like structured siloxanes have side chains that resemble the teeth of a comb, providing enhanced surface interactions. Linear structured siloxanes have a straight chain configuration, offering improved flexibility and stability. Combined structured siloxanes incorporate both comb-like and linear structures, combining the benefits of each for specific applications in the market.</p></p>
