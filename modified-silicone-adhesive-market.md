<p><p>Modified Silicone Adhesive includes two main types: Neutral Cure and Moisture Cure. Neutral Cure adhesives do not release any byproducts during the curing process, making them suitable for use on sensitive substrates. Moisture Cure adhesives, on the other hand, cure by reacting with moisture in the air, making them ideal for outdoor applications where moisture is present. Both types offer strong adhesion and flexibility, making them popular choices for various industrial and construction applications.</p></p>
