<p><p>Propylene Glycol Ether (PGE) market consists of three main types: PM (Propylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether), DPM (Dipropylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether), and TPM (Tripropylene Glycol Mono Methyl Ether). PM is a solvent with high evaporation rate and excellent solvency power, commonly used in industrial coatings. DPM is a slow-evaporating solvent suitable for coatings requiring low volatility. TPM is a high-boiling point solvent with good solvency and low toxicity, often used in cleaner formulations. Each type serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
