<p><p>Single exhaust systems consist of one exhaust pipe attached to the engine, whereas dual exhaust systems have two separate exhaust pipes coming from the engine. Single exhaust systems are more common and cost-effective, while dual exhaust systems provide better performance and efficiency by allowing for smoother airflow. Dual exhaust systems are often found in higher-performance vehicles, as they can increase horsepower and improve the overall driving experience. Ultimately, the choice between a single and dual exhaust system depends on the driver's preferences and budget.</p></p>
