<p><p>The Aluminum Alloy Fasteners market consists of various types of fasteners such as Aluminum Nuts, Washers, Screws, Bolts, Countersunk Screws, and Tapping Screws. Aluminum Nuts are used to fasten components together, while Washers help distribute the load of a fastener. Aluminum Screws and Bolts are used for mechanical fastening, whereas Countersunk Screws are designed to sit flush with the surface. Tapping Screws are used for creating internal threads in materials like aluminum. Each type of fastener serves a specific purpose in various industries.</p></p>
