<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Organic Sport Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic</li><li>Hypotonic</li><li>Hypertonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic Sport Drink Market Types are categorized based on their osmolarity levels, which determine how quickly the drink is absorbed by the body. Isotonic drinks have a similar concentration of salt and sugar as the body's fluids, making them quickly absorbed and ideal for rehydration during exercise. Hypotonic drinks have a lower concentration of salt and sugar, making them absorbed more slowly and suitable for mild hydration needs. Hypertonic drinks have a higher concentration of salt and sugar, providing a quick energy boost but slower hydration.</p></p>
