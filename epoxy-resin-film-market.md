<p><p>Epoxy resin film is available in two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent epoxy resin films are clear and allow light to pass through, making them ideal for applications where visibility is important. Opaque epoxy resin films, on the other hand, are solid and do not allow light to pass through, providing a more opaque finish. Both types of epoxy resin films offer different aesthetic and functional qualities, catering to a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
