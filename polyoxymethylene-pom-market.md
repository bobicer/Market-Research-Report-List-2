<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM), also known as acetal, is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic with excellent strength, stiffness, and low friction properties. There are two main types of POM markets: homo-polyoxymethylene, which consists of chains of the same repeating unit, and co-polyoxymethylene, which contains two different repeating units in the polymer chain. Homo-polyoxymethylene is more commonly used in applications requiring high mechanical strength and stiffness, while co-polyoxymethylene is preferred for applications where improved thermal stability and chemical resistance are necessary.</p></p>
