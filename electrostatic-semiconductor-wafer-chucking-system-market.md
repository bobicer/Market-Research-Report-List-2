<p><p>Electrostatic Semiconductor Wafer Chucking System market includes two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. </p><p>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks use the principle of electrostatic attraction to hold semiconductor wafers in place during processing. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize a combination of Coulomb forces and electrophoresis to achieve strong wafer holding capabilities.</p><p>Both types of chucking systems play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
