<p><p>The UF Resins Market is segmented into Type I and Type II based on the formaldehyde to urea molar ratio. Type I resins have a higher formaldehyde content and lower urea content, making them suitable for applications requiring high moisture resistance and durability. Type II resins have a lower formaldehyde content and higher urea content, making them better suited for applications requiring low emission, such as interior wood products. These two types cater to different industry needs and market demands.</p></p>
