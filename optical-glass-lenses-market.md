<p><p>Optical glass lenses are classified into two main types: aspherical and non-aspherical. Aspherical optical glass lenses have a more complex shape that helps reduce optical aberrations and improve image quality. They are widely used in cameras, telescopes, and microscopes. On the other hand, non-aspherical optical glass lenses have a simpler shape and are commonly found in eyeglasses and magnifying glasses. Each type caters to different applications and industries, offering distinct benefits based on their design and functionality.</p></p>
