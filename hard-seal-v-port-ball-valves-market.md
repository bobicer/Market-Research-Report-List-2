<p><p>Hard seal V port ball valves are available in two main market types: flangedless and flanged. Flangedless valves do not have flanges on the body and are designed for direct welding to a pipeline, making them more compact and suitable for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, flanged valves feature flanges that allow for easy installation and removal, making them ideal for applications that require frequent maintenance or where disassembly is necessary. Both types offer reliable performance and long service life for a variety of industrial applications.</p></p>
