<p><strong>The Automotive Ball and Roller Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Bearings</li><li>Roller Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive ball bearings are designed to support radial and axial loads, reducing friction between two rotating surfaces. They consist of inner and outer rings with precision balls between them to enable smooth rotation. On the other hand, roller bearings use cylindrical or tapered rollers to support heavy radial loads. They are more suitable for applications with higher load capacities and are commonly used in automotive transmissions and wheels. Both ball and roller bearings play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of various automotive components.</p></p>
