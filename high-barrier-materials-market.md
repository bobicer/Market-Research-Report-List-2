<p><p>High barrier materials refer to packaging materials that provide excellent protection against oxygen, moisture, and other external factors that can degrade or spoil the contents. The three main types of high barrier materials in the market are PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol), and PEN (polyethylene naphthalate). PVDC is known for its exceptional gas and moisture barrier properties, EVOH offers high oxygen barrier capabilities, and PEN provides superior temperature resistance and barrier performance, making them popular choices for food, pharmaceutical, and other sensitive product packaging applications.</p></p>
