<p><strong>The Car Smart Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) use sensors in the vehicle to detect the presence of the key fob, allowing the driver to unlock or lock the car without pressing any buttons. Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES) require the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or lock the vehicle. Both systems offer convenience and security benefits for drivers, but PKES is considered more advanced as it requires less manual action from the driver.</p></p>
