<p><p>Draw Wire Encoders are devices used to measure linear movement in various applications. Absolute Draw Wire Encoders provide a unique digital code for each position along the measurement range, allowing for accurate and precise measurement. On the other hand, Incremental Draw Wire Encoders produce a series of pulses to track the movement, requiring a reference point for starting position determination. Both types of encoders are popular in industries such as automation, robotics, and automotive for position control and monitoring applications.</p></p>
