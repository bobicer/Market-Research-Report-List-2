<p><p>The Light Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper market consists of two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have only one piston that applies pressure to the brake pads, while Multi-Piston Calipers have multiple pistons for more evenly distributed pressure and better braking performance. Single Piston Calipers are typically more cost-effective and simpler in design, while Multi-Piston Calipers offer greater stopping power and improved heat dissipation. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
