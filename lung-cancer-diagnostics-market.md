<p><p>The Lung Cancer Diagnostics Market includes various types of diagnostic techniques, such as Immunoassays, Flow Cytometry, Rapid Tests, Molecular Assays, Tissue Arrays, Circulating Tumor Cells, Pharmacodiagnostics, Biomarkers, and Other market. Immunoassays detect cancer-related proteins in blood samples, while Flow Cytometry analyzes cells for cancer markers. Rapid Tests provide quick results, and Molecular Assays analyze genetic material for cancer mutations. Tissue Arrays examine multiple tissue samples simultaneously, while Circulating Tumor Cells detect cancer cells in blood. Pharmacodiagnostics tailor treatment based on a patient's genetic makeup, and Biomarkers indicate the presence of cancer. Other market technologies include imaging techniques and liquid biopsies.</p></p>
