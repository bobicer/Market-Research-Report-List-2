<p><p>Industrial UV inkjet ink for multi-pass printers and single-pass printers are two main types of ink used in the industrial printing market. Multi-pass printers use UV ink that is optimized for printing on various substrates with high resolution and color vibrancy. On the other hand, single-pass printers require UV ink that can dry instantaneously as the printhead passes over the substrate only once. Both types of ink are essential for different printing applications in the industrial sector, catering to unique printing requirements and production speeds.</p></p>
