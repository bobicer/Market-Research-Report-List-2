<p><p>The indoor fiber optic cables market consists of three main types based on the number of cores: single core, double core, and multi-core. Single core cables contain one glass core for transmitting data, making them ideal for simple point-to-point connections. Double core cables have two cores for higher capacity and redundancy. Multi-core cables have multiple cores and are used for complex networks requiring multiple data connections. Each type serves different purposes depending on the requirements of the indoor environment.</p></p>
