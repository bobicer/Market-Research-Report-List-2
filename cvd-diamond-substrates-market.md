<p><p>CVD diamond substrates are categorized into four main types based on their thickness: below 20 µm, 20 µm - 100 µm, 100 µm - 300 µm, and above 300 µm. Each market type caters to different applications and industries. Substrates below 20 µm are typically used in electronics and semiconductors, while those between 20 µm - 100 µm are commonly found in optics and thermal management. Substrates ranging from 100 µm - 300 µm are used in cutting tools and wear-resistant applications, while substrates above 300 µm are primarily utilized in high-pressure research and engineering.</p></p>
