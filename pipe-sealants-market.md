<p><p>Pipe sealants are available in three main types based on their viscosity: Medium Viscosity, High Viscosity, and Higher Viscosity. Medium Viscosity sealants are ideal for general purpose applications, while High Viscosity sealants are better suited for sealing larger gaps and preventing leaks. Higher Viscosity sealants are designed for heavy-duty applications where a strong bond is required. Each type offers specific benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the project or application.</p></p>
