<p><p>Flexible epoxy resins are commonly found in three different types: Rubber Modified, Urethane Modified, and Dimer Acid. Rubber Modified resins provide increased flexibility and impact resistance. Urethane Modified resins offer improved adhesion and resistance to chemicals. Dimer Acid resins are known for their excellent heat and chemical resistance. Each type of flexible epoxy resin caters to different specific applications and requirements, providing a wide range of options for various industries such as construction, automotive, electronics, and coatings.</p></p>
