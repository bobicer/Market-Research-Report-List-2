<p><p>Corrosion resistant rebar market consists of various types such as epoxy-coated rebar, galvanized rebar, glass-reinforced-plastic rebar, stainless steel rebar, MMFX rebar, and low alloy corrosion resistant rebar. Stainless steel rebar is highly resistant to corrosion. MMFX rebar contains micro alloying elements for increased durability. Low alloy corrosion resistant rebar is made of materials with lower susceptibility to corrosion.</p></p>
