<p><p>Ferrite beads are commonly used in electronic circuits to suppress high-frequency noise. The market for ferrite beads can be segmented into two main types: Ferrite chip beads and through-hole beads. Ferrite chip beads are surface-mount components that are commonly used in compact electronic devices due to their small size and high efficiency in noise suppression. Through-hole beads, on the other hand, are larger components that are soldered onto a circuit board for noise suppression in various applications. Both types of ferrite beads are essential in maintaining signal integrity and minimizing interference in electronic circuits.</p></p>
