<p><p>High density bamboo is used for various applications including flooring, furniture, and construction materials. The market types for high density bamboo include cold pressing and hot pressing methods. In cold pressing, the bamboo is compressed at room temperature, resulting in a denser and more durable product. On the other hand, hot pressing involves subjecting the bamboo to high temperatures during compression, which also increases its density and strength. Both methods have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the end product.</p></p>
