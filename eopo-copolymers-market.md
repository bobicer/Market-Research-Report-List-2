<p><p>EO/PO copolymers are classified based on the percentage of ethylene oxide (EO) in the polymer chain. The different market types include 10% EO, 20% EO, 30% EO, 40% EO, 50% EO, 70% EO, 80% EO, and Others. Higher EO content in the copolymer results in increased hydrophilicity and solubility, making them suitable for applications such as surfactants, emulsifiers, and lubricants. Lower EO content copolymers are more hydrophobic and find applications in areas such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Each percentage range offers specific properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
