<p><p>5G Non-Standalone (NSA) Architecture Infrastructure includes various market types such as Femtocell, Pico Cell, Micro Cell, and Macro Cell. Femtocells are small, low-power cellular base stations designed for use in residential or small business environments. Pico cells are slightly larger than Femtocells and are typically used to increase capacity in indoor environments. Micro cells cover larger outdoor areas such as city blocks or shopping malls. Macro cells are the largest and most powerful, covering wide outdoor areas such as cities or rural regions.</p></p>
