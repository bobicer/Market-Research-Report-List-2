<p><p>Acrylic resins are widely used in various industries and are categorized into three main types: Methacrylates, Acrylates, and Hybrids. Methacrylates are known for their excellent weatherability and impact resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Acrylates offer strong adhesion properties and are often used in adhesives and coatings. Hybrids combine the best properties of both Methacrylates and Acrylates, creating a versatile option for multiple applications. Each type of acrylic resin offers unique benefits and properties, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
