<p><strong>The Life Jacket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent life jackets</li><li>Inflatable life jackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inherent life jackets are designed to provide buoyancy without needing to be inflated, making them a reliable and easy-to-use option for water safety. They are typically made of foam or other buoyant materials that keep the wearer afloat. In contrast, inflatable life jackets rely on a CO2 cartridge or manual inflation to provide buoyancy when needed. They are more compact and comfortable to wear, but require maintenance to ensure they function properly in an emergency. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the life jacket market.</p></p>
