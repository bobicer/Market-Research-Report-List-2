<p><strong>The IoT Communication Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular Communication Module</li><li>Non-cellular Communication Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IoT communication modules serve as the backbone of IoT networks and can be broadly classified into two types: cellular and non-cellular communication modules. Cellular communication modules use cellular networks like 4G or 5G to transmit data wirelessly, making them suitable for applications requiring high-speed and reliable connections. Non-cellular communication modules, on the other hand, rely on technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or Zigbee for connectivity, making them more suitable for short-range and low-power applications. Both types cater to various IoT use cases with different connectivity requirements.</p></p>
