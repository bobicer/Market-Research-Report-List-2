<p><p>Modular carpet tiles are a popular flooring option due to their versatility and ease of installation. The market is divided into two main types: flexible modular carpet tiles and rigid modular carpet tiles. Flexible tiles are soft and pliable, making them ideal for high-traffic areas where comfort is key. Rigid tiles, on the other hand, are more durable and are better suited for areas with heavy furniture or high levels of foot traffic. Both types offer a wide range of colors, patterns, and textures to suit any design aesthetic.</p></p>
