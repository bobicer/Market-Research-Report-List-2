<p><p>Automotive Active Seatbelt market includes various types such as Belt-In-Seat, Six-Point Belt, Five-Point Belt, Four-Point Belt, Three-Point Belt, and Two-Point Belt. Belt-In-Seat provides additional safety by integrating the seatbelt into the seat itself. Six-Point Belt offers enhanced protection with straps across both shoulders, hips, and between the legs. Five-Point Belt secures the body with straps across both shoulders, hips, and between the legs. Four-Point Belt offers protection with straps across both shoulders and hips. Three-Point Belt secures the body with a strap across the shoulders and lap. Two-Point Belt provides basic protection with a lap belt only.</p></p>
