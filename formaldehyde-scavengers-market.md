<p><p>Formaldehyde scavengers are used to eliminate formaldehyde emissions in various industries and applications. The types of formaldehyde scavengers include neutralization reaction, catalytic decomposition, oxidation-reduction, and sealing plugging. Neutralization reaction involves a chemical reaction that neutralizes formaldehyde molecules. Catalytic decomposition breaks down formaldehyde into harmless byproducts. Oxidation-reduction involves the transfer of electrons to reduce formaldehyde levels. Sealing plugging creates a barrier to prevent formaldehyde from escaping. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences in the formaldehyde scavengers market.</p></p>
