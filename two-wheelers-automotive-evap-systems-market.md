<p><p>The Two Wheelers Automotive EVAP Systems market consists of various components such as fuel tanks, gas caps, liquid vapor separators, control valves, sensors, and hoses. The fuel tank stores the gasoline, while the gas cap prevents fuel from evaporating. The liquid vapor separator separates liquid fuel from vapor. The control valve regulates the flow of fuel vapor. Sensors monitor the EVAP system for leaks or malfunctions. Finally, hoses connect the different components of the EVAP system to ensure proper functioning.</p></p>
