<p><p>Vacuum sputter equipment market includes various types such as DC sputtering, RF sputtering, and others. DC sputtering utilizes a direct current to create a plasma for sputtering materials onto a substrate, while RF sputtering uses radio frequency power to generate the plasma. Other types may include magnetron sputtering or ion beam sputtering. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of process control, film quality, and target material options, catering to different industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
