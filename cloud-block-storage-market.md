<p><p>Cloud Block Storage offers various market types such as Locally-attached Storage and Network-attached Storage. Locally-attached Storage is directly connected to a single server or virtual machine, providing high performance but limited scalability. On the other hand, Network-attached Storage allows multiple servers to access the storage simultaneously over a network, offering greater scalability and flexibility. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of businesses, depending on their performance and scalability requirements.</p></p>
