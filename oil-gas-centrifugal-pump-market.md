<p><p>Oil & Gas Centrifugal Pump Market includes various types such as Axial Flow Pumps which move fluid parallel to the pump shaft, Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps which combine features of both axial and radial flow pumps, Peripheral Pumps with impellers designed to create high pressure at low flow rates, and Jet Pumps which utilize the Venturi effect to boost fluid flow. Each type serves a different purpose in the oil and gas industry, from transporting fluids over long distances to providing high pressure for well stimulation.</p></p>
