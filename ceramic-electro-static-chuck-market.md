<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Electro Static Chuck Market Types include Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chucks rely on the attraction between positive and negative charges to hold the wafer in place, while JR Type chucks use a combination of Coulomb force and Van der Waals force. Coulomb Type chucks are known for their strong holding force, making them suitable for high-speed applications, while JR Type chucks offer a more gentle grip, reducing the risk of wafer breakage during handling.</p></p>
