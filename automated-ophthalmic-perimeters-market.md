<p><strong>The Automated Ophthalmic Perimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Ophthalmic Perimeters</li><li>Kinetic Ophthalmic Perimeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Ophthalmic Perimeters are devices used to measure visual fields in patients. There are two main types of Automated Ophthalmic Perimeters: Static Ophthalmic Perimeters and Kinetic Ophthalmic Perimeters. </p><p>Static Ophthalmic Perimeters test the central and peripheral vision by presenting stimuli at fixed locations on a screen. Kinetic Ophthalmic Perimeters, on the other hand, use moving stimuli to evaluate visual field defects. Both types of perimeters are essential tools in diagnosing and monitoring conditions such as glaucoma, retinal disorders, and neuro-ophthalmic diseases.</p></p>
