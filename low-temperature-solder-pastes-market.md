<p><ul><li>Silver Contained</li><li>Silver-free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low temperature solder pastes are available in two main types: silver contained and silver-free. Silver contained pastes typically have a lower melting point due to the addition of silver, making them ideal for applications where less heat is desired. Silver-free pastes, on the other hand, do not contain silver and rely on alternative materials to achieve low melting temperatures. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various industries depending on the specific requirements of the soldering process.</p></p>
