<p><p>Flexible Manufacturing System Solutions market can be categorized into different types based on the level of flexibility and customization they offer. Sequential FMS follows a predetermined sequence for manufacturing tasks. Random FMS allows for flexible task allocation without a set sequence. Dedicated FMS is designed for specific tasks or products. Engineered FMS are customized solutions developed for specific production needs. Modular FMS consist of interchangeable modules that can be reconfigured to adapt to different manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
