<p><p>2D Gesture Recognition market includes two main types: Static Gesture Recognition and Dynamic Gesture Recognition. Static Gesture Recognition involves recognizing hand gestures or poses without any movement, such as holding up a peace sign or pointing. This type is commonly used for simple interactions like controlling a PowerPoint presentation. Dynamic Gesture Recognition, on the other hand, involves recognizing gestures that involve movement, such as swiping, waving, or pinching. This type is often used in gaming and virtual reality applications for more complex interactions.</p></p>
