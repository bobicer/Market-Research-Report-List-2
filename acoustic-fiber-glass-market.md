Continuous fiber is long strands of glass fibers used for continuous manufacturing processes. Fixed length fiber consists of short strands of glass fibers used for specific applications. Glass wool is made from fine fibers of glass used for thermal and acoustic insulation. Each type of acoustic fiber glass serves a different purpose in soundproofing and insulation applications.</p></p>
