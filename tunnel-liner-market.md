<p><p>Tunnel liner market types are classified into Type I and Type II based on their application and material properties. Type I tunnel liners are designed for use in soft soil conditions and are typically made of materials such as corrugated metal or polyethylene. On the other hand, Type II tunnel liners are specifically designed for more challenging conditions like rocky terrain and are usually made of more durable materials like concrete or steel. Both types of tunnel liners play a crucial role in providing structural support and ensuring the stability of tunnels.</p></p>
