<p><p>Flame retardant polypropylene (PP) can be classified into two main types based on the type of flame retardants used: Halogen flame retardant PP and Halogen-free flame retardant PP. </p><p>Halogen flame retardant PP contains halogen-based compounds, such as bromine or chlorine, which help inhibit the spread of flames. On the other hand, Halogen-free flame retardant PP uses alternative flame retardants that do not contain halogen elements. </p><p>Both types of flame retardant PP are used in various applications where fire safety is crucial, such as in electronics, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
