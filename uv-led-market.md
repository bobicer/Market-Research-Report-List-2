<p><strong>The UV-LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV-LED market is segmented into three main types based on the wavelength of light emitted: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs emit long-wave ultraviolet light, commonly used in applications like curing, tanning, and counterfeit detection. UV-B LEDs emit medium-wave ultraviolet light, used in medical applications such as phototherapy. UV-C LEDs emit short-wave ultraviolet light, known for their disinfection capabilities and commonly used in water and air purification systems. Each type of UV-LED serves specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
