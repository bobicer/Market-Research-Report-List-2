<p><p>Aviation actuator system market consists of three main types - electromechanical, electro hydraulic, and electro hydrostatic. Electromechanical actuators utilize electric motors to generate motion, while electro hydraulic actuators use hydraulic fluid to drive the mechanism. Electro hydrostatic actuators combine the benefits of both systems, using hydraulic fluid under high pressure to transmit power through an electrically driven pump. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications within the aviation industry.</p></p>
