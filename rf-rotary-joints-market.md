There are three main types of RF rotary joints available in the market - Single Channel, Double Channel, and Multi-Channel. Single Channel rotary joints transmit signals through one path, Double Channel rotary joints through two separate paths, and Multi-Channel rotary joints through multiple paths simultaneously. Each type caters to different requirements based on the number of signal paths needed in the RF system.</p></p>
