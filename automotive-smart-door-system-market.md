<p><p>The Automotive Smart Door System market includes two main types: Automated Controlled System and Electronically Controlled System. The Automated Controlled System uses sensors and actuators to automatically open and close doors in response to certain triggers, such as key fob proximity. On the other hand, the Electronically Controlled System relies on electronic signals from the user, such as pushing a button on a key fob or a smartphone app, to operate the doors. Both systems aim to provide convenience and enhance the overall user experience.</p></p>
