<p><p>Analog Signal Boosters and Digital Signal Boosters are two main types of Mobile Network Boosters available in the market. Analog Signal Boosters amplify the traditional analog signals to enhance mobile network coverage, while Digital Signal Boosters work by amplifying and retransmitting the digital signals to improve signal strength. Both types of boosters are designed to eliminate signal interference and ensure better call quality and faster data speeds for mobile users. Customers can choose the type of booster based on their specific network requirements and preferences.</p></p>
