<p><p>A non-isolated high voltage DC-DC converter is a type of converter that does not provide electrical isolation between the input and output. It is typically used in applications where isolation is not required, such as in automotive or industrial settings. On the other hand, an isolated high voltage DC-DC converter provides electrical isolation between the input and output, making it suitable for applications where safety and protection from electrical hazards are important, such as in medical devices or telecommunications equipment.</p></p>
