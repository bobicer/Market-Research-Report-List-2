<p><p>The Automotive Engine Bracket market consists of two main types: Passive Elastomeric Brackets and Active Engine Brackets. Passive Elastomeric Brackets are designed to absorb vibrations and reduce noise generated by the engine, providing a stable and smooth driving experience. Active Engine Brackets, on the other hand, use sensors and actuators to actively adjust engine mounting position in real-time, further enhancing vehicle performance and comfort. Both types play a crucial role in supporting and securing the engine within the vehicle chassis.</p></p>
