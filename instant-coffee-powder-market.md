<p><ul><li>Spray Dry Coffee</li><li>Freeze Dry Coffee</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instant coffee powder market can be categorized into two main types: spray dry coffee and freeze dry coffee. Spray dry coffee is made by spraying hot, concentrated coffee into hot air which quickly evaporates the water content, resulting in fine coffee powder. On the other hand, freeze dry coffee involves freezing the concentrated coffee before drying it, preserving more of its original flavor. Both types of instant coffee powder offer convenience and quick preparation, making them popular choices among consumers.</p></p>
