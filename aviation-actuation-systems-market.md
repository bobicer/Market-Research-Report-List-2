<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Drive System</li><li>Electric Drive System</li><li>Pneumatic Drive System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation actuation systems market types include hydraulic drive systems, electric drive systems, and pneumatic drive systems. Hydraulic drive systems utilize fluid power to generate motion, providing high force output and precise control. Electric drive systems use electrical energy to drive actuators, offering quieter operation and easier maintenance. Pneumatic drive systems use compressed air to power actuators, providing fast response times and simple control mechanisms. All three systems play crucial roles in aircraft control and movement, catering to different needs and preferences in the aviation industry.</p></p>
