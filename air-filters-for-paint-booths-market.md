<p><p>Air filters for paint booths come in two main types: Coarse filters and HEPA/ULPA filters. Coarse filters are designed to capture larger particles like dust and debris, preventing them from entering the paint booth and potentially contaminating the finish. On the other hand, HEPA/ULPA filters are highly efficient at capturing even the smallest particles, ensuring a clean and contaminant-free environment for painting. Both types of filters are essential in maintaining air quality and achieving high-quality finishes in paint booth operations.</p></p>
