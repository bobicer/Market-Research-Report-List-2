<p><p>VCI Film market consists of two main types: VCI Stretch Film and VCI Shrink Film. VCI Stretch Film is used to securely wrap around objects, providing protection against corrosion, moisture, and other damages during storage or transportation. It has the flexibility to conform to different shapes and sizes. On the other hand, VCI Shrink Film is designed to shrink tightly around products when heated, creating a sealed barrier that protects against rust and other environmental factors. Both types of VCI Film are essential for preserving the quality of goods in various industries.</p></p>
