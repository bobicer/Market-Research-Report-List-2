<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pressure-sensitive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Hot Melt & Radiation</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure-sensitive adhesive market is categorized into four main types: water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, and radiation-curable adhesives. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but release harmful VOCs. Hot melt adhesives provide quick bonding and are commonly used in packaging. Radiation-curable adhesives use UV or electron beam radiation for curing. Other types of adhesives include acrylic, silicone, and rubber-based adhesives, each offering unique bonding properties for various applications.</p></p>
