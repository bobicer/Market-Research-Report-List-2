<p><p>Antenna positioner market can be divided into different types based on their movement capabilities. Azimuth positioners allow antennas to rotate horizontally, while vertical XY positioners enable antennas to move in vertical and horizontal directions. Horizontal XY positioners offer movement in two horizontal directions. Other types of positioners may include combination of these movements or additional features. Each type caters to specific requirements of antenna positioning in various applications such as satellite communication, radar systems, and wireless networks.</p></p>
