<p><p>The Automotive Adaptive Front Lighting System consists of two main types: High Beam and Dipped Headlight. The High Beam feature provides increased visibility on dark roads by projecting a stronger beam of light ahead of the vehicle. On the other hand, the Dipped Headlight feature automatically adjusts the angle and intensity of the light based on the vehicle's speed and the surrounding environment to prevent blinding other drivers. Both features enhance safety and visibility for drivers, especially in low-light conditions.</p></p>
