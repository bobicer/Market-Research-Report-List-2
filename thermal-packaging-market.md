<p><p>Thermal packaging refers to the use of materials that are designed to maintain a consistent temperature within a packaged product, protecting it from temperature fluctuations during transportation or storage. These materials, such as insulated liners, gel packs, and phase change materials, help to keep perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and other sensitive products at the required temperature.</p><p>The global thermal packaging market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for temperature-sensitive products, such as biopharmaceuticals and specialty foods, as well as the growing e-commerce industry and the need for efficient cold chain logistics. Additionally, stringent regulations regarding the transportation of temperature-sensitive goods are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the thermal packaging market include the development of sustainable and recyclable insulation materials, the integration of technology such as temperature-monitoring sensors in packaging solutions, and the adoption of reusable packaging options to reduce waste. Overall, the increasing focus on maintaining product quality and safety during transportation and storage is expected to drive the growth of the thermal packaging market in the coming years.</p></p>
