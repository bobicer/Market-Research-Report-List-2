<p><p>Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT) are typically categorized into two main types - Darrieus and Savonius. Darrieus turbines have a unique design with aerodynamic blades that are curved like an eggbeater, allowing them to efficiently capture wind from any direction. On the other hand, Savonius turbines have a simple design with curved blades that rotate when wind hits them. Both types of VAWTs are popular in the market for their ability to generate renewable energy in urban and remote areas.</p></p>
